I love to read !

Reading is an efficient way of traveling worlds without leaving my chair

I love reading, though I hesitate to call myself a bookworm. When I'm not caught up in the daily bustle, I find joy in exploring materials that spark my curiosity—ranging from politics and fiction to entertainment, religion, and materials on tech innovation. There's a saying that a reader lives a thousand lives; perhaps I haven’t yet, but I’m certainly on the path. The beauty of reading lies in its power to inform, expand one’s horizons, introduce new ways of expressing thoughts, and—perhaps most importantly—keep the mind sharp.
